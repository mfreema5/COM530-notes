##[Tumblr themes](https://www.tumblr.com/docs/en/custom_themes)

**Seven key post types:**
* text
  * `{block:Text}`
  * `{/block:Text}`	
* photos
  * `{block:Photo}`
  * `{/block:Photo}`
* links
  * `{block:Link}`
  * `{/block:Link}`
* quotes
  * `{block:Quote}`
  * `{/block:Quote}`
* chat
  * `{block:Chat}`
  * `{/block:Chat}`
* audio
  * `{block:Audio}`
  * `{/block:Audio}`
* video
  * `{block:Video}`
  * `{/block:Video}`
